    The first question people always ask is: Why do you think a music festival in Zürich will work? It makes us smile; it’s the one question we can so easily answer!!

    • Switzerland is ranked #1 in the world for quality of life, and boasts outstanding natural beauty, hospitality, fine cuisine, sports, recreation, cleanliness, environ-mental quality, a multi-cultural mind-set, technology, infrastructure, high security, and dreams come true.
    • Switzerland is rich in music: Montreux Jazz Festival, Interlaken Classical Music Festival, Lucerne Festival, Paléo Festival, Zurich Film Festival, Zurich Opernhaus and the Tonhalle, the Hallenstadion, stunning lakeside concerts and more.
    • Switzerland regularly attracts global gi-ants like the Rolling Stones, Celine Dion, Lady Gaga, Sting, Madonna, Justin Bieber – and is home for Tina Turner.
    • ZIMF will be the first annual indoor mul-ti-genres music festival spanning two weekends.
    • ZIMF will support charities, foundations and NGOs creating interest globally.
    • Zurich offers great access and a central location, easy to reach by train, plane or car from anywhere and everywhere.
    • Expected attendance: 80K-100K, which is in line with other major music festivals.
